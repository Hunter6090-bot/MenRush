Why MenRush isn't a grid. And isn't an app.

Hey,

Quick one. Grindr's grid is the most copied UI in dating-app history. We're not copying it.

Here's why.

A grid sorts men by distance. Two columns, scroll forever. It's efficient — and it's exactly the same experience whether you're at home, in a bar, or 30 miles into the countryside.

A live map is different. You don't sort men. You see them. Where they actually are. In a coffee shop two streets over. In the bar you're heading to. On the bus moving toward you.

Proximity becomes context. Context creates intent. Intent creates real meetings.

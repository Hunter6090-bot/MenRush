Quick story.

A while back I was at a bar. Decent night. I opened Grindr — same screen everyone's seen. Nothing happening. Three guys in the bar, all of them on the app, none of us talking. We all left separately.

That moment kept bothering me.

The apps aren't broken because of bad design. They're broken because they show a cached list of who once downloaded the app — not who's actually here, right now, looking.

That's what MenRush is fixing.

What MenRush Fixes

→ Live presence, not stale profiles

→ Map-first, not feed-first

→ Who's actually nearby, actively online

→ No swipe queues, no message walls, no waiting

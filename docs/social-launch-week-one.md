# MenRush Social Launch: Week One

Start date: 2026-06-02  
Primary CTA: `https://menrush.com`  
Approval model: draft first, human approve before publish.

## Positioning

- MenRush is for men who know what they want.
- The hook is speed, proximity, and less noise.
- The conversion goal is always the waitlist.
- Early waitlist members get 30 days of Premium free when MenRush launches.
- Keep the tone direct, premium, adult, confident, and not spammy.
- Every scheduled visual post should use approved MenRush media. Default poster: `https://menrush.com/menrush-logo.png`.

## Cadence

| Channel | Daily minimum | Best first test windows, UK time | Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|
| X | 2 posts | 08:30 and 19:30 | Founder voice, build-in-public, fast hook testing. |
| Instagram | 1 feed post or story | 19:30 | Schedule through Metricool. Use logo or approved campaign poster. |
| TikTok | 1 draft or post | 18:30 to 21:00 | Manual-first. Use hooks as short scripts, not polished ads. |
| Reddit | 1 contribution every 2 to 3 days | 12:00 to 20:00 | Ask real questions. Do not drop links where it reads as spam. |
| Bluesky | 1 post | 13:00 | Keep it crisp, conversational, and lower-pressure than X. |

Daily operator loop:

1. Approve today's copy and asset by 10:00 UK.
2. Queue Instagram in Metricool as a draft first.
3. Post X AM manually or via approved automation, then watch replies for 30 minutes.
4. Publish or schedule Bluesky around 13:00.
5. Use TikTok hook as a 15 to 25 second founder-style clip or text-on-screen concept.
6. Log shipped posts, link clicks, replies, follows, profile visits, saves, Reddit comments, and waitlist signups.

## Metricool Import Guidance

Use Metricool for Instagram scheduling first. Keep all rows as drafts until approved.

Required CSV columns based on the existing import:

```csv
Text,Date,Time,Draft,Facebook,Twitter/X,LinkedIn,GBP,Instagram,Pinterest,TikTok,YouTube,Threads,Bluesky,Picture Url 1,Alt text picture 1,Instagram Post Type,Shortener,Brand name
```

Recommended defaults:

- `Draft`: `TRUE`
- `Instagram`: `TRUE`
- all other platform booleans: `FALSE` for Instagram-only imports
- `Picture Url 1`: `https://menrush.com/menrush-logo.png` unless an approved campaign poster is ready
- `Alt text picture 1`: `MenRush official logo`
- `Instagram Post Type`: `POST`
- `Shortener`: `FALSE`
- `Brand name`: blank unless Metricool requires the connected brand label

Example row:

```csv
"Built for men who know what they want.

No endless noise. No pretending. No waiting around for a maybe.

MenRush is coming.

Join the waitlist at menrush.com",2026-06-02,19:30:00,TRUE,FALSE,FALSE,FALSE,FALSE,TRUE,FALSE,FALSE,FALSE,FALSE,FALSE,https://menrush.com/menrush-logo.png,MenRush official logo,POST,FALSE,
```

Import checklist:

1. Open Metricool and choose the MenRush brand.
2. Import CSV into planner.
3. Confirm timezone is Europe/London before final scheduling.
4. Confirm image preview renders.
5. Leave as draft until human approval.
6. After approval, schedule Day 1 to Day 3 only. Keep Day 4 to Day 7 editable until early performance data comes in.
